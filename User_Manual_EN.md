<summary> Admin Tab </summary>

- You can only create, edit, and delete quizzes if you have the proper permissions. If you only have user role the "edit photoshoots" tab will not show up.
- To create a photoshoot you will have to go in the edit photoshoots tab, then press the button create new booking, this will then open a modal where you can write in all the necessary details for the booking.
  <img width="1727" height="1155" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a22301c9-6f7d-45d1-af9b-8fbd5bb31792" />
  <img width="491" height="485" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/eec8600e-652e-4218-9f6d-73b88fded874" />


- Then you can add more details if you want in the panel on the side
<img width="1841" height="556" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/30bbbd63-e2f2-4c90-9aa0-d48571b95521" />
  
- To delete a photoshoot you will have to delete the corresponding details for that photoshoot first. Simply press the "x" on the right side of the grid 
<img width="1798" height="354" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1352b170-3bea-481b-9abb-3bf15f5321de" />

- When you have created your Photoshoot it will appear in the Bookings Tab, and you can enter it.

- Under all this is a persons bookings tab grid where you can see all the bookings that have been made. An admin is able to delete all bookings.
  <img width="1546" height="538" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ada237f0-6057-471f-bfd9-13f6d445f194" />

<summary> How To Book </summary>

- Start in the calendar app and choose a date that fits for you, note that if a date is marked as red that means that day is fully booked and you cant choose that date.
<img width="2526" height="466" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/81a72663-1c8d-4bc1-a1d1-901dfbfd868f" />

- when you have selected your date click the go to booking button.

- You can then see the date you picked and how many avaliable bookings there are left for this day
  <img width="1737" height="313" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b7a4760a-5491-4360-8fd7-18ebc7afd25e" />

- Click the go to booking button to see the booking for which ever type of photoshoot you want
- You will then be sent to the booking details page where you can choose which bookings fits the best, and you can leave a comment if desired. When you click book you will get a notification saying 'this timeslot is already booked' if someone has already chose this timeslot.
- But if its not taken you get the message "sucessfully booked"
<img width="1424" height="488" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3692dc6c-b23d-4942-9394-469f7c36b438" />


- you then get sent to back to the photo bookings page where you can then view your booking in the my bookings tab
<img width="1902" height="491" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2237b207-954d-4a94-91b4-e18355d6ee4e" />

- You also get an SMS confirming your booking! If you dont have a phone number attached to your account dont worry, it will then send a mail confirming the booking. 
<img width="1080" height="987" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b40d21d2-9404-4925-a24f-7678142c7d47" />


- If you regret your booking you can easily cancel it by clicking the "cancel booking" button 

- The booking will also be inserted in the persons bookings grid that is in the edit photoshoots so admins can easily review the booking that has just been made.
